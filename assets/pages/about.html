Our Story

A brief background

Retrenchment from Santam Structured Insurance –Credit Insurance Division due to closure due to this tumultuous economic environment, has led to the start of a Credit Management Consultation business between these two partners.

The main reason for starting the business was that there is a real need for companies who cannot afford credit insurance, who need to be guided in obtaining the best credit management solution to grow their business while optimizing profits.
We believe that our invaluable experience and network of contact built up over the years will assist any company in achieving success in the management of their cashflow to unlock debtor value while managing their creditors effectively.
